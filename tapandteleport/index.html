Tap and Teleport

How long can you survive?

Tap and Teleport is a 3D reaction-based survival game.

You control a lone wizard with the power of teleportation! Use this power to dodge a variety of hazards: fireballs, archers, soldiers, lancers, and even fellow mages. Challenge yourself to survive and collect orbs for points and power-ups. Utilize your reaction skills to survive as you challenge your high-score.

How to play:

Tap anywhere in the arena to teleport your wizard. Use this teleportation power to dodge hazards. But watch out! Each time you teleport, you drain your limited supply of energy. Collect orbs to recharge your energy and score points. The more energy you have, the more points you will score. Survive as long as possible and beat your high-score!
