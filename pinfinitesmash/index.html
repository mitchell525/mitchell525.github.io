Pinfinite Smash

Endless pinball brick breaker!

Coming soon!

Pinfinite Smash is a 2D pinball game combined with an endless brick breaker.

In this retro themed pinball game, you will face endless waves of bricks in need of breaking! Watch out, as the brick speed increases as you progress! Take advantage of powerful upgrades to help clear your way, earning increased point rewards to help you top your high score!

Are you up to the challenge? As you top your high score, you can even unlock new skins for your pinball!

Pinfinite Smash can be enjoyed casually and offline: don't let an airplane or bus ride keep you from topping your high score!

Features:

  • Simple and intuitive gameplay
  • 2D pinball action
  • PowEndless brick breakererups
  • Randomized Power-ups
  • Unlockable skins
  • Realistic physics
  • Offline single player
