Simplified logging for your daily run

Jog it Log it is a simple logging app, perfect for recording your daily exercise without confusing setup or nonsense bells and whistles. Track your progress and get fit!

Jog it Log it features a simple and intuitive interface for entering your daily run. You can view multiple charts of your exercise data to track your progress overtime.
