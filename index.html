Mitch Smith

Mitchell (Mitch) Smith is a full time Software Engineer and father from Baltimore Maryland. In his free time he enjoys creating apps as a hobby.

These are some of the apps I have developed, check them out and let me know your thoughts!

Pinball Overdrive

Pinball Overdrive is a retro-themed pinball game inspired by the arcade era!

Watch out! Various moving, bouncing, and exploding hazards make reaching the next level a challenge! Come back and play again to explore more randomized levels, gain a score multiplier or bonus pinballs to top and share your high score!

Pocket Travel Planner

Pocket Travel Planner is a simple, free, and nonintrusive app designed to help you plan every aspect of your journey with ease. From organizing itineraries to packing lists and countdowns, Pocket Travel Planner keeps your trip details all in one place.

Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or an epic adventure, Pocket Travel Planner makes trip planning easy!

Pinfinite Smash

Pinfinite Smash is a 2D pinball game combined with an endless brick breaker.

In this retro themed pinball game, you will face endless waves of bricks in need of breaking! Watch out, as the brick speed increases as you progress! Take advantage of powerful upgrades to help clear your way, earning increased point rewards to help you top your high score!

Tap and Teleport

Tap and Teleport is a 3D reaction-based survival game.

You control a lone wizard with the power of teleportation! Use this power to dodge a variety of hazards: fireballs, archers, soldiers, lancers, and even fellow mages. Challenge yourself to survive and collect orbs for points and power-ups. Utilize your reaction skills to survive as you challenge your high-score.

Pinball Defense Force

Pinball Defense force is a classic 2D pinball game inspired by the arcade era!

In this retro pinball game you will face endless waves of randomized enemies as you defend space with your pinball and paddles. Come back again and again, as no two games are the same. Explore randomized enemy formations, defeat challenging bosses, take advantage of powerful upgrades, and earn score multipliers to top your high score!

Surge Blast

Launch your way into Surge Blast! This hyper casual game allows you to release and match bubbles of different colors to score points.

Match five bubbles of the same color to score points and survive as long as possible! But watch out! Every eight bubbles, an increasingly difficult blast of different colors will try to disrupt your efforts!

Bounce and Bound

Jump into Bounce and Bound, a free, retro-themed puzzle game inspired by the arcade era!

Work your way through 36 challenging levels. Strategically drop bouncing balls to hit three goals and move onto the next level! But watch out! Various moving, bouncing, and exploding hazards make finding the correct time and location to drop a challenge.

Dizzy Frog

A ultra casual color hopping game.

Help Dizz the Dizzy Frog leap from lily pad to lily pad! If you’re successful, Dizz changes colors and new lily pads appear in endless randomized patterns; the fun never stops! The more accurate your jump, the more points you gain! Play over and over again to build your high score!

Shift and Shatter

Shift and Shatter is a quick-paced color matching game. There are six different game modes to choose from, each featuring fun, retro styles inspired by the arcade era.

Match shapes by color. But be careful; make sure to avoid bombs and the walls! Challenge yourself to improve your reflexes and beat your high score!
