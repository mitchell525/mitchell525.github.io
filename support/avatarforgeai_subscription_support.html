Use this guide to quickly resolve common subscription issues. If a step doesn't apply to you, skip ahead.

1. Quick fixes (try these first)

  • Open the app > Store tab > tap "Restore Purchases" and wait 10â€“20 seconds
  • Make sure you are signed into the correct Apple ID on this device (Settings > [your name])
  • Force-quit the app and reopen it; if needed, restart your iPhone

2. "Subscription linked to another account" message

What it means

  • Your subscription is active on this device's Apple ID but is already linked to a different AvatarForge AI account (email) in our system. Each subscription can only be attached to one app account at a time.

How to fix

  • If you own the subscription under a different app account: Sign Out in the app, then Sign In with the account that originally subscribed
  • If you want to move the subscription to a new app account: Cancel the current subscription in the App Store, wait until it expires, then subscribe again from the desired account in the app
  • You can also manage your subscription directly: App Store > Account > Subscriptions > AvatarForge AI

3. Restore Purchases isn't restoring my subscription

Checklist

  • Confirm the Apple ID: The Apple ID on your device must match the one that purchased the subscription
  • Tap "Restore Purchases" in the app Store tab and wait (don't close the app during restore)
  • If you installed via TestFlight previously, install the App Store build and sign in again (TestFlight and App Store entitlements are separate)
  • If you recently changed Apple ID regions or payment method, re-open the App Store app, then try restore again

4. Which app account owns my subscription?

  • The subscription is linked to the first AvatarForge AI account that successfully synced after purchase
  • For privacy and security, we cannot disclose another user's identity
  • If you have multiple emails, try signing in with each one and tap "Restore Purchases" to find the linked account

5. Find your Apple Subscription/Transaction details

  • Open Mail and search for "Apple Receipt" for the AvatarForge AI subscription
  • Or go to: App Store > Account (top-right) > Your Account > Purchase History > Subscriptions
  • Look for "Original Transaction ID" or the subscription line item and note the date and product

6. Billing issues or lapsed subscription

  • Update your payment method: Settings > [your name] > Payment & Shipping
  • If billing temporarily failed, Apple may place your subscription in a billing retry or grace period; once resolved, your access restores automatically
  • If fully expired, tap "Restore Purchases" after fixing your payment method, or re-subscribe

7. Cancel or change your plan

  • Manage via App Store: App Store > Account > Subscriptions > AvatarForge AI
  • Changes take effect immediately or at the next renewal depending on Apple's policies

8. Family Sharing

  • Family Sharing for auto-renewable subscriptions may not grant in-app entitlements for all family members. Our app links a subscription to a single in-app account. Each user should subscribe with their own in-app account if shared access isn't available

9. TestFlight vs App Store

  • TestFlight subscriptions and App Store subscriptions are separate environments. If you tested on TestFlight, you may need to install from the App Store and subscribe/restore there

10. Still need help?

In the app, go to Settings > Help/Support and include: your app account email, Apple ID (do not send password), purchase date, and any receipt details (e.g., Original Transaction ID). We will investigate and guide you through next steps.
