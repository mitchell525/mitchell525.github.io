Shift And Shatter

Color Matching Game

iOS Android
Shift and Shatter is a quick-paced color matching game. There are six different game modes to choose from, each featuring fun, retro styles inspired by the arcade era.

Match shapes by color. But be careful; make sure to avoid bombs and the walls! Challenge yourself to improve your reflexes and beat your high score!
