Surge Blast

Blasting Matching Fun

iOS

Launch your way into Surge Blast! This hyper casual game allows you to release and match bubbles of different colors to score points.

Match five bubbles of the same color to score points and survive as long as possible! But watch out! Every eight bubbles, an increasingly difficult blast of different colors will try to disrupt your efforts!

Are you up to the challenge? Play Surge Blast, and prove you have what it takes to hold the high score against your friends!
